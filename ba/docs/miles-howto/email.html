🚗 New: log your whole day's miles as one trip

Hey team — the mileage logger in the BA app just got a big upgrade. Your day's driving is now one journey made of legs (Home → store → store → home), instead of separate one-off trips. Here's the 2-minute tour.

1) Your day is one mileage card

On your home screen, each day now shows a single blue Mileage card with your route and total miles. Tap it any time to view or edit the day.

2) Log miles one leg at a time

Tap Log miles. Leg 1 starts from Home. Type your destination and pick the store from the list (that's what links the trip to the account). Hit Calculate miles — or type them — pick a category, and Save leg 1. The next leg opens automatically, starting wherever you just stopped. Repeat until you're home.

3) Review the day at a glance

Opening a day shows every leg chained together. Tap a leg to edit it, hit ✕ to delete one (it asks first), or ＋ Add another leg if you kept driving.

4) Categories: pick all that apply

Category is now multi-select — a stop can be "Store visit" + "Demo / PAD". Check everything that applies, then Done. And if a drive wasn't store work at all, just pick Lead, Non-BA activity, or General BA Activity/Admin — those trips don't need a store link (the old "* Lead / * Non-BA activity / * General BA activity" destinations are gone — use the category instead).

5) Anything missing glows red

If a trip is incomplete, it shows in your To-do list and the day card gets a ⚠. Tapping it jumps straight to the leg that needs attention, with the missing fields outlined in red — they clear as you fill them in.

Quick tips
  • Odometer people: the Odometer tile still works — snap start & end photos and the app reads the numbers for you.
  • Nav screenshot: upload your Maps screenshot and the app reads the miles off it.
  • Always pick the store from the suggestion list rather than free-typing — that's what ties your miles to the account.
  • If the app looks different from these pictures, close and reopen it (or tap the "New version" bar) to get the update.
